# Product PRD: Fulfillment-aware discovery ranking

## Problem

Search CTR is **17.09%** at ranks 1–5 but only **1.91%** below rank 10. The detected curve cliff is near rank **11**. Zero-result rates peak in categories where discovery quality and late-period GMV are weakest.

## Proposal

Add a bounded fulfillment-quality boost to the relevance score for active listings whose sellers have fulfillment ≥90%, strong ratings, and low historic exposure. Preserve query relevance as the primary score and cap the boost so poor matches cannot leapfrog relevant inventory.

## Success metrics

- Primary: purchase-through rate per search; target relative lift 5%.
- Secondary: CTR, zero-result rate, GMV per search, and exposure share for qualified sellers.
- Guardrails: refunds, p95 page latency, seller complaints, and category concentration.

## Expected value and rollout

Moving 8% of buried impressions toward top-rank performance produces a directional **1.21% absolute CTR lift** and **$2,316 monthly GMV opportunity**. Validate at 5% traffic, then 25%, before full rollout. Required sample size is approximately **73,844 users per arm** for 80% power at α=.05, an 8% baseline purchase rate, and 5% relative lift.
